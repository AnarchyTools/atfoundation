=================
String extensions
=================

Swift strings are very powerful with their complete unicode support, but as they do not appear as ``SequenceType`` anymore we're lacking some of the simpler manipulation functions. *atfoundation* tries to supply you a solution for that that is done safely and as fast as possible in pure Swift.
